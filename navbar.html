Bike modifications involve customizing a motorcycle's aesthetics, comfort, or performance. While minor cosmetic changes, vinyl wraps, and ergonomic tweaks (like grips or seats) are largely hassle-free, major structural or engine alterations often require official RTO approval. Always prioritize safety-certified parts and notify your insurer to keep your policy valid.
